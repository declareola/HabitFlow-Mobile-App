### **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

**Product Name:** HabitFlow AI
**Tagline:** Your AI-powered operating system for habits, focus, wellness, and growth.
**Vision:** To build a unified ecosystem where habit tracking, deep work, wellness metrics, and AI coaching converge to drive measurable personal growth.

**Target Audience & Market:**
Designed to support a diverse, global user base, the platform targets Young Professionals, Remote Workers, Students, and Entrepreneurs. The total addressable market intersects productivity, wellness, and AI coaching, exceeding $500B in potential.

**Core Engines & Features:**

* **Habit Engine:** Daily/weekly tracking, automated streak management, and AI-driven recovery plans for missed days.
* **Focus Engine:** Customizable timers (Pomodoro, Deep Work) integrated with energy prediction models.
* **Wellness Engine:** Comprehensive tracking for sleep, hydration, exercise, meditation, and mood.
* **AI Coach:** Proactive, predictive insights connecting cross-engine data to offer personalized recommendations.
* **Gamification Engine:** A 7-tier leveling system (Beginner to Legend) fueled by XP, badges, and streaks.

**Key Performance Metrics (The Science)**
The platform will utilize proprietary algorithms to quantify growth.

Habit Strength Formula:


$$Habit\ Strength = Consistency \times Frequency \times Duration$$

Wellness Score Formula:


$$Wellness\ Score = 0.3(Sleep) + 0.25(Exercise) + 0.2(Hydration) + 0.15(Meditation) + 0.1(Mood)$$

Productivity Score Formula:


$$Productivity\ Score = \left(\frac{Completed\ Sessions}{Planned\ Sessions}\right) \times 100$$
