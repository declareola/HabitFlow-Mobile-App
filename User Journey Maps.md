### **User Journey Maps: Design & UX Handoff**

These maps dictate exactly how the UI should feel, focusing on reducing friction and maximizing the "Aha!" moment.

#### **Journey 1: The "Aha!" Onboarding**

*Goal: Get the user to experience the AI's empathy within the first 3 minutes.*

1. **Screen 1 (Welcome):** "What brings you to HabitFlow?" (Options: Reduce Burnout, Build Discipline, Track Health).
2. **Screen 2 (The Baseline):** "Let's set your baseline. How many hours did you sleep last night?" (User inputs data).
3. **Screen 3 (First Insight):** The screen transitions to a chat interface. The AI Coach appears: *"Got it. Operating on less than 6 hours of sleep means your deep focus will naturally dip this afternoon. Let's set a simple goal for today: Drink 2 liters of water and try one 25-minute focus session. Ready?"*
4. **Result:** The user instantly realizes this is not a rigid tracker, but an adaptive partner.

#### **Journey 2: The Recovery Loop (Failing a Habit)**

*Goal: Turn a missed habit into a retention opportunity rather than a reason to uninstall.*

1. **Context:** The user misses their "Morning Workout" for two consecutive days.
2. **Screen 1 (The Notification):** Instead of a red "You failed" alert, a push notification fires: *"Energy looking a bit low lately? Let's adjust."*
3. **Screen 2 (The AI Pivot):** The user opens the app. The AI Coach suggests a pivot: *"I noticed the morning workouts aren't happening. No stress. Would you like to pause that habit for the week, or switch it to a 10-minute evening stretch instead?"*
4. **Result:** The user selects the evening stretch. The streak is maintained under "Recovery Mode," and the user feels supported, eliminating the guilt that usually causes churn.

#### **Journey 3: Global Timezone Mastery**

*Goal: Seamless operation for digital nomads and global teams.*

1. **Context:** An executive assistant travels from Lokoja to London for a conference.
2. **Action:** The app detects the device timezone change.
3. **Screen 1 (Smart Sync):** A subtle banner appears: *"Timezone shift detected. Updating your habit deadlines to local time. Your current streaks are protected."*
4. **Result:** Complete peace of mind. The user's data remains perfectly synced without manual recalibration.
